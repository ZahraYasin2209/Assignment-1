Ingredients:

For a Chocolate Cake

  • all-purpose flour
  • sugar
  • unsweetened cocoa powder
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • espresso powder
  • milk
  • oil
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • boiling water

How to Bake a Cake??

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat the oven to 350ยบ F.Prepare two 9-inch cake pans by spraying with baking spray or buttering and lightly flouring.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients: Add flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt and espresso powder to a large bowl or the bowl of a stand mixer.
  3. Mix in wet ingredients: Add milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla to flour mixture and mix together on medium speed until well combined.
  4. Addition of boiling water: Reduce speed and carefully add boiling water to the cake batter until well combined.
  5. Bake: Distribute cake batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Frost: Remove from the oven and allow to cool for about 10 minutes, remove from the pan and cool completely. Frost with Chocolate Buttercream Frosting.
